P3 - Grocery Shopping



Description:
This is a simple Grocery Shopping app. It allows you to add different lists for particular items. It works like the Notes app on IOS. Once you create a Grocery List, you may click on the cell and edit the Ingredients you want to pick up from the store. You can swipe to the left of either the list or item to delete them from the table.  


Extra Features*: Swipe to delete, WebView, Gestures/Swiping on Front page.


Inspiration and Sources:  

Inspired and learned from Rob Percival for the Grocery List, CoreData, and Webviews

P2 - The Food Finders 2



Description:
Food Finder is an app that makes deciding on what to eat a lot easier! On the welcome screen users can click on either image to decide whether they want to dine in at a restaraunt, or cook at home! When selecting to dine in, the user will be shown their current location, and places to eat. Choosing to cook yourself will give you a editable grocery list, and a cooking timer.


Extra Features: Searching Map, Pin drops with Restaurant Info, and Swiping


Inspiration and Sources:  

Inspired and learned from Rob Percival for the maps, grocery list, and countdown timer.
